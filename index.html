Welcome to Drutance

We are dedicated to raising awareness and combatting antimicrobial resistance (AMR).

About Us

Drutance is a non-profit organization committed to understanding, preventing, and battling antimicrobial resistance (AMR). We collaborate with experts, researchers, and communities to ensure a healthier future for everyone.

Our Projects

  • Education & Awareness: Campaigns to spread knowledge about AMR.
  • Research Collaboration: Partnering with leading institutions to drive innovation.
  • Community Engagement: Grassroots efforts to reduce the spread and impact of AMR.

Support Our Cause

Every donation helps us move one step closer to a world free from the threats of antimicrobial resistance.
